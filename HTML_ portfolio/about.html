About us

I'm Abdullahi Olakunle Abdullahi, a result-driven Aeronautical and Astronautical engineer concentrating on aerodynamic and mechanical design, Computer-Aided Design (CAD), and Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD). With a solid foundation in engineering principles and practical project experience, I am committed to expanding the limits of what can be achieved in aviation and aerospace.

I am a graduate of Aeronautical and Astronautical Engineering from Kwara State University, currently i'm pursuing an MSc in Software Engineering at GOMYCODE School of Technology/Woolf (Oct 2024 – Present), I am expanding my skill set into software development, front-end web technologies, and data analysis, reinforcing my ability to integrate engineering and technology solutions.

I have gained hands-on experience through roles such as an NYSC Intern at the Ministry of Innovation, Science, Technology, and Digital Economy, where I worked on telecommunications infrastructure and solar energy systems, and as an Internship Engineer at the Nigerian Air Force’s 307 Executive Airlift Group, where I assisted in aircraft maintenance and system inspections. Additionally, as a Project Lead at Kwara State University (2022 – 2023), I spearheaded the design and development of a High-Altitude Long-Endurance UAV, performing CFD simulations, stress analysis, and leading a team of engineers.

My proficiency in CAD software enables me to produce accurate and effective designs that combine both functionality and creativity. I am particularly motivated by the challenges of optimizing aerodynamic forms to improve performance and efficiency. Through my experience in CFD, I have gained a deep understanding of fluid dynamics and utilize simulation tools to examine complex flows, enhance designs, and guarantee high-performance results.

I continually seek opportunities to learn and contribute to projects that address real-world challenges in aerospace. Whether through collaborative efforts or independent research, my aim is to make a significant contribution to the field of aerospace engineering.
